# ROADMAP: THERMODYNAMIC STRUCTURALISM

## The Theory of Structural Realization (TSR)

**Objective:** Define the architecture of the coupling between Mathematics and Physics. We are not unifying them; we are describing the **Layer of Realization** that selects which mathematical structures can exist as physical processes.

> **Core Thesis:** "Mathematics is the space of possible designs. Physics is the set of realized designs. Between them lies the **Filter of Realizability**, governed by thermodynamics, causality, and spectral stability."

## üìú THE AXIOMS OF REALIZABILITY (Minimal)

A mathematical structure $S$ is **Physically Realizable** iff it satisfies:

### R1. Informational Finitude

$K(S) < \infty$. The structure must admit a finite description. Prohibits non-constructive reals.

### R2. Causal Executability

The structure must admit a local, causal update rule. No global instantaneous propagation.

### R3. Spectral Stability

$\exists \Delta_{min} > 0$. The realization must be robust to perturbation (Gap check).

### R4. Thermodynamic Cost

Energy $E(S)$ and Time $\tau(S)$ must not diverge instantly. Execution respects Landauer Limit.

### R5. Physical Readout

The result must be physically distinguishable. (Kills "Magic" Quantum Shortcuts).

## ü™ú THE HIERARCHY OF REALIZABILITY (HRE)

We classify mathematical branches by their physical cost:

### üü¢ Class R0 ‚Äî Weakly Realizable

- Finite Sets, Boolean Algebra, Automata.
- **Cost:** Low. Trivial.

### üü° Class R1 ‚Äî Conditionally Realizable

- Polynomial Algorithms (P), Classical Mechanics (Approx).
- **Cost:** Linear/Polynomial. Stable.

### üîµ Class R2 ‚Äî Asymptotically Realizable

- Quantum Mechanics, Spectral Geometry (Riemann).
- **Cost:** High. Requires isolation.
- **Conjecture:** **Riemann Hypothesis characterizes a maximally stable R2 structure.**

### üî¥ Class R3 ‚Äî Ideally Realizable (Limit)

- Perfect Qubits, Exact Symmetries, Continuum.
- **Status:** Only exists as a limit $N \to \infty$.

### ‚ö´ Class NR ‚Äî Non-Realizable (Under Axioms R1-R5)

- NP-Complete in Poly-Time, Uncomputable Reals, Infinite Sets.
- **Definition:** Structures that allow no realization with sub-exponential cost under physical laws.
- **Status:** Censored by the Universe. **P=NP lives here.**
