We demonstrate that the previously defined five terms of the action functional (Geometry, Matter, Reaction, Saturation, and Boundary) are not independent components but emergent limits of a single unified informational flow. Until now, the Tamesis Action was a "Master Theory of Regimes"—a highly successful unification of General Relativity, Quantum Mechanics, and MOND. However, the presence of five distinct terms suggested an underlying multiplicity. In this final derivation, we discard the multiplicity in favor of a single variation.
