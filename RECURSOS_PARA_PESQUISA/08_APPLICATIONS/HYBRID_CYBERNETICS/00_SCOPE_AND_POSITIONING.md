# Scope & Positioning: Hybrid Cybernetics

**Field:** Thermodynamic Stability of Hybrid Cognitive Systems
**Context:** Information Theory, Control Theory, Complex Systems
**Status:** Theoretical Foundation

---

## 1. The Core Problem: Informational Instability

We address a singular, measurable problem affecting modern cognitive systems: **Inference Collapse under Information Saturation.**

As the cost of generating information approaches zero (via Generative AI) and the volume of available data exceeds the thermodynamic processing capacity of biological agents ($I_{flow} \gg C_{bio}$), the coupled system (Human + Tool) risks entering unstable regimes characterized by:

1. **Hallucination Amplification:** Feedback loops where AI errors are validated by saturated human operators.
2. **Model Collapse:** Degeneration of synthetic training data quality.
3. **Epistemic Paralysis:** Inability to distinguish signal from noise due to overload.

**Hybrid Cybernetics** is the study of the physical and control-theoretic conditions required to maintain **stability** (bounded error rates) in these hybrid systems.

## 2. What This Field Is (and Is Not)

To ensure institutional legitimacy and rigorous falsifiability, we strictly delimit the scope:

| Aspect | **THIS IS (Hybrid Cybernetics)** | **THIS IS NOT (Speculation)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Object of Study** | Error rates, Entropy production, Channel capacity. | "Consciousness", "Sentience", "Qualia". |
| **Methodology** | Information Theory (Shannon), Control Theory (Wiener). | Philosophy of Mind, Critical Theory. |
| **Goal** | Stability theorems, optimization protocols. | AGI/ASI alignment, "Superintelligence". |
| **Operators** | Stochastic (LLM) vs. Deterministic (Human) nodes. | "Minds" vs. "Machines". |

**Key Distinction:** We treat Humanity not as a "sacred biological essence" but as a **High-Precision / Low-Bandwidth Operator** essential for grounding the floating-point drift of **Low-Precision / High-Bandwidth Operators** (LLMs).

## 3. Position Applied to Other Fields

### vs. Traditional Cybernetics (Wiener/Ashby)

Traditional cybernetics focused on homeostasis in biological or mechanical systems. We extend this to **Semantic Homeostasis** in systems where the "regulator" (AI) has higher variance than the "plant" (Reality).

### vs. AI Safety / Alignment

AI Safety often deals with normative values ("Do what I mean"). Hybrid Cybernetics deals with **Thermodynamic Constraints** ("Can this system physically sustain truth?"). We argue that "Alignment" is a special case of "Thermodynamic Stability".

### vs. The Free Energy Principle (Friston)

We adopt the mathematics of Active Inference (minimizing variational free energy) but apply it specifically to the **interface** between two distinct types of inference engines (Biological vs. Silicon) rather than a single brain.

## 5. First Principle (The Hypothesis)

> **The Hybrid Stability Hypothesis:**
> A coupled system $H \circ M$ (Human-Machine) can achieve a global entropy production rate $\dot{S}_{total}$ lower than either component in isolation, *if and only if* the coupling architecture enforces specific bandwidth constraints on the $M \to H$ channel.

If this hypothesis holds, we can derive the laws of optimal collaboration from physics, not management theory.
