Information Symmetry Breaking: The Origin of Structure

Douglas H. M. Fulber
Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
(Dated: January 23, 2026)
We introduce the concept of "Information Symmetry". A state is information-symmetric if all basis vectors are equiprobable (Maximum Entropy, zero structure). Structure formation is the spontaneous breaking of this symmetry. We show that the universe began in a state of high Information Symmetry (Uniform Plasma) and is evolving towards a state of maximal specific complexity.

Structure is the corpse of broken symmetry.

The Order Parameter

We define the order parameter $\psi$ as the deviation from the uniform distribution:

$$ \psi = 1 - \frac{S}{S_{max}} $$

As the universe expands, $S_{max}$ (Holographic Bound) grows faster than $S$ (Matter Entropy). $\psi$ grows from 0 to 1. This "Entropy Gap" is the potential energy that drives complexity.
