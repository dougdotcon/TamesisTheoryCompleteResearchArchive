2. Why RH Was Not Proven

To prove RH via the Hilbert-Pólya approach, one must:

  1. Define a self-adjoint operator $H$ on a proper domain
  2. Prove $D(H) = D(H^*)$ rigorously
  3. Show $\det(s - H) = \xi(s)$ analytically
  4. Conclude that eigenvalues are real (hence zeros on critical line)

We did none of these. We discretized a matrix and computed eigenvalues numerically.
