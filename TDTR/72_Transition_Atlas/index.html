An Atlas of Physical Transitions

Douglas H. M. Fulber
Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
(Dated: January 23, 2026)
We compile the first comprehensive "Atlas of Transitions," mapping the known boundaries between physical regimes. We catalogue topological phase transitions, universality class shifts, and dimensional reductions. This Atlas serves as the periodic table of the TDTR framework, allowing for the prediction of new, composite states of matter at the interfaces of known theories.

Map the edges, and you define the territory.

Discussion

The existence of these sharp boundaries confirms the granular nature of physical law. Future work will focus on the Tamesis Transition $U_0 \to U_{1/2}$, which offers the most promise for technological application.
