1. The Central Object

$$D = -i \cdot x \cdot \frac{d}{dx}$$

Acting on $L^2(\mathbb{A}_\mathbb{Q} / \mathbb{Q}^*)$

This is the Connes dilation operator. It is the generator of the scaling flow $\lambda \mapsto \lambda^t$ on the adelic quotient.

Key property: If $f$ is an eigenfunction with eigenvalue $\gamma$:

$$D f = \gamma \cdot f \implies f(\lambda x) = \lambda^{i\gamma} f(x)$$

2. Connection to Riemann Zeros

Connes' Program: $$\text{Spec}(D) \longleftrightarrow \{\gamma_n : \zeta(1/2 + i\gamma_n) = 0\}$$

The spectrum of $D$ encodes the Riemann zeros.

The eigenfunction $x^{i\gamma}$ has eigenvalue $\gamma$. Verification:

$$D(x^{i\gamma}) = -i \cdot x \cdot (i\gamma) \cdot x^{i\gamma - 1} = \gamma \cdot x^{i\gamma}$$
