Systematic Comparison: Tamesis vs. String Theory vs. LQG

Douglas H. M. Fulber
Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
(Dated: January 23, 2026)
We conclude our program by comparing Tamesis against its rivals. Unlike Strings (too many vacua) or LQG (no continuum limit), Tamesis is both rigid and predictive. It predicts $\alpha = 1/137.036$ (Calculable) and $M_c = 10^{-15}$ kg (Falsifiable). It is the only theory that provides a closed-form derivation of the Standard Model parameters.

The winner is the one that predicts the number.
