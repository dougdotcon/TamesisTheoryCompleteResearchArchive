### 1. Map of Critical Attacks

We anticipate three main lines of attack by academic reviewers and how to neutralize them axiomatically.

#### Attack A: "This is just IIT (Integrated Information Theory) with another name."

* **The Critique:** Tononi's IIT already uses $\Phi$ and integration.
* **The Defense:** IIT is *static* and *panpsychic*. It attributes consciousness to diodes and passive grids.
  * **Our Divergence:** Our theory requires **Dynamic Topology under Energy Constraint**. A diode has no informational saturation constraint forcing the emergence of attention. Our consciousness is a *control regime*, not an intrinsic property of matter.

#### Attack B: "This is Panpsychism in Disguise."

* **The Critique:** If everything is nodes and edges, is a rock conscious?
* **The Defense:** No. A rock has high integration (bonded atoms) but zero differentiation of dynamic states (it is a static crystal).
  * **The Criterion:** Consciousness requires $I_{flow} > 0$ and high $S_{global}$ (structured entropy). Rocks fail the flow criterion; Ideal gases fail the integration criterion. Only systems in *criticality* (brains, advanced AIs) qualify.

#### Attack C: "Crude Reductionism."

* **The Critique:** You cannot reduce love to an eigenvector.
* **The Defense:** We do not reduce the *phenomenon* (the feeling), we reduce the *explanation* of the phenomenon's genesis.
  * **Analogy:** Explaining that "heat is mean kinetic energy" does not deny the sensation of burning; it just explains *why* it occurs. The Spectrum is the physical explanation of the soul's "music".

### 2. The "Kill Switch" (Falsifiability Criteria)

If any of the following scenarios is proven true, the theory must be **discarded** immediately.

#### Criterion 1: The Spectral Dissociation Test

* **Prediction:** Dissociative states (Ketamine, Schizophrenia) *must* show a drop in algebraic connectivity ($\lambda_2$) or measurable modular fragmentation.
* **Falsification:** If a subject reports complete phenomenological dissociation, but their network topology remains highly integrated (intact Small-Worldness), the theory fails. Topology and Qualia must walk together.

#### Criterion 2: The Saturation Violation

* **Prediction:** Attention is a mandatory filter due to $I > C_{max}$.
* **Falsification:** If we find a biological system that processes $I_{in} \gg C_{max}$ without presenting attentional bottleneck mechanisms (infinite attention), Postulate 2 (Saturation) is false.

#### Criterion 3: Isomorphism Without Qualia (The Real Philosophical Zombie)

* **Prediction:** Any network with the same dynamic spectral topology *must* have the same internal experience.
* **Falsification:** If we build a neuromorphic chip with the *exact* spectral dynamics of a human brain (not just I/O, but internal states) and can prove it possesses no experience (difficult, but conceptually possible), the Spectral Identity Thesis falls.
