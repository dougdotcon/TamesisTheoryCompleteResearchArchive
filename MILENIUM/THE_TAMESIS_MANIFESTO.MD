### 1. The Core Thesis

The Millennium Prize Problems are commonly understood as "missing proofs" for objects that we intuitively believe exist (or don't).
**We propose a different view:** They are **Obstruction Problems**.

They represent the precise boundary where **Abstract Logic** (which allows infinite pathologies) conflicts with **Structural Stability** (which imposes thermodynamic, geometric, or arithmetic rigidity).

> **Thesis:** *Mathematical Pathologies (Singularities, Magic Algorithms, Ghost Cycles) are not "undecidable mysteries". They are structurally unstable configurations that are censored by the natural operators of the theory (Dissipation, Coercivity, Variance).*
