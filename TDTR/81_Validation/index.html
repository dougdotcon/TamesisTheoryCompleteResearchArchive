Validation of the TDTR Framework against Standard Benchmarks

Douglas H. M. Fulber
Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
(Dated: January 23, 2026)
We test the validity of the Thermodynamic Time Reversal (TDTR) theory by ensuring it recovers all standard results of established physics in the appropriate limits. We show that TDTR reduces to General Relativity in the limit of large horizon area, recovers Quantum Mechanics in the limit of zero gravity, and reproduces the Friedmann equations for an isotropic universe.

A new theory must not only explain the new; it must respect the old.
