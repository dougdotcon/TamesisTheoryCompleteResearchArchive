4. Analysis

Why $\Lambda$ Works (Marginally)

$\Lambda = 3/R_H^2$ with $R_H = c/H_0$ is essentially the definition of the de Sitter horizon. This is a tautology, not a derivation.

Why $a_0$ Fails

The "MOND coincidence" $a_0 \sim c \cdot H_0$ is an empirical observation with ratio ~0.18 (not ~1). Calling this a "derivation" is misleading.

Why $M_c$ Fails Catastrophically

$M_c$ depends on LOCAL quantum physics:

  • Coherence length (~$10^{-6}$ m)
  • Self-gravitation of quantum superpositions
  • Diosi formula: $M_c \sim (\hbar R_{\rm coh} / G)^{1/3}$

$M_c$ does NOT contain $R_H$, $\Lambda$, $H_0$, or any cosmological scale.
